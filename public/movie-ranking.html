The Best Movies According to Sixto

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

Heat

Now, what can be better than a movie including Robert De Niro Vs. Al Pacino right?. So Heat is the perfect movie to watch pretty much anytime, everywhere... a lot of action, tough guys against each other, and two brilliant minds against each other. The movie is more than 3 hours i think, and you never wanna get out of the TV.

Collateral

Collateral is a great movie with a great story, a lot of violence, good actors and is always driving you into suspense!

Point Break

Point Break is a hell of a movie because i love surfing!, great actors, and a super hilarious story!
